===================
Connect from Looker
===================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

You can import data from a MongoDB collection into the Looker BI Platform
with the {+download-center-bi+}. You do not need an ODBC data connection or
a System DSN.

Prerequisites
-------------

Connecting the Looker BI Platform to the |bi-atlas| requires the
following:

- An M10+ Atlas cluster with the |bi-short| enabled

- A `Looker <https://looker.com/product/business-intelligence>`_ account
